# Pattern Detection Rules

Additional patterns beyond simple keyword matching.

## Urgency Signals

**Patterns:**
- "urgent", "need gone", "quick sale"
- "moving", "relocating", "need space"
- "buyer fell through", "sale fell through"

## Negotiation Stance

**Firm price signals:**
- "firm", "no offers", "price is firm"
- "fixed price", "as advertised"

**Open to negotiation:**
- "open to offers", "make an offer"
- "negotiable", "willing to negotiate"

## Swap/Trade Indicators

**Keywords:**
- "swap", "trade", "trade in", "part exchange"
- "will consider swap for", "open to trades"

## Maintenance Evidence Patterns

**Service history:**
- "full service history", "complete service history"
- "logbook", "service book", "service records"
- "receipts available", "all receipts"

**Workshop evidence:**
- "serviced at [workshop]", "dealer serviced"
- "maintained at [shop]"

## Missing Information Patterns

Look for phrases indicating gaps:
- "not sure", "unknown", "don't know"
- "hasn't been used for [time]", "been sitting"
- "previous owner", "don't have history"
